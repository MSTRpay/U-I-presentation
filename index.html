Market Gap & Opportunity

The Gap

  • Limited cross-border solutions for SMEs
  • No integrated lifestyle banking ecosystem
  • Underserved women and informal entrepreneurs
  • High device access barriers

The Opportunity

  • Lead in super-app digital finance
  • Capture remittances & lifestyle revenue
  • Onboard 150,000+ new users in Year 1
  • Expand to MRO licensing for mobile money

Strategic Insight

With Vision 2030, we can capture 15% of digital lending market in 24 months, leveraging U & I’s client base.

How This Partnership Wins

A 50/50 JV combines MSTRpay’s tech with U & I’s market leadership for mutual growth.

For U & I

  • Access to MSTRpay ecosystem without CAPEX
  • €3/share per onboarded user + €0.50/customer equity top-up
  • 50/50 profits; national expansion via tech
  • Path to MRO or Class A bank license

For MSTRpay

  • Access to thousands of U & I clients
  • Office space in Nairobi for 4 employees
  • Regulatory compliance via CBK license
  • Insights into women/SME markets

Strategic Insight

Aligned incentives: We provide tech, U & I offers trust/infrastructure, unlocking €600k Y1 revenue.
